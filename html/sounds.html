The sound of Music!icker

Adaptive music and sound effects with Wwise audio engine

Sounds are the core of the game and they are split in two categories: music and sound effects.
For the music I wrote a simple song with very simple chords and I recorded instruments that I had at home and for the ones that I didn't have I used free VSTi downloaded from the web. The Wwise audio engine did handle the tempo and the signature and with an index representing the total number of unlocked musicians (from 0 to 24) if could control the volume of each instrumens as they were unlocked and added to the mix.
Adding the voice was the mosti difficult part. It had to play a random note chosen from the currently played chord, and for that I recorded some variation of the same note (C) and created some random containers with different pitches that would play a random sound from the recorded ones at a random pitch from the chord ones. During the playback the chord change and with that the voice plays always the correct note.
For the sound effects I used simple things that I had at home, like the computer mouse clik, hand clapping, classical guitar effects or downloaded animal sounds.
Here you can listen to some tracks and to some SFX. Play the game for discovering how everything works together!
