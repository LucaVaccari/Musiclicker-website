Music!icker

A music theme Android idle game

Music!icker is an Android game developed completely by a 16 year old guy with Unity game engine. In the game you are a singer that gain notes as he sings. With the notes he can level up, gaining more notes. He can also hire musicians to play with him and produce even more notes. When he reaches the level 60, he'll be able to fight the bad music in the final battle.
